About MindConnect

MindConnect is a centralized platform designed to support university students' mental health and wellbeing. We understand how overwhelming campus life can be, and our mission is to make it easier for students to access trusted counseling services, community events, and support groups—all in one place.

By combining thoughtful design, reliable data, and user-driven features, MindConnect empowers students to take control of their mental health journey and build resilience, confidence, and community.

Meet the Creator

  • Favour Fatusin: Founder and Developer — Favour created MindConnect out of a passion for making mental health resources more visible and accessible. As a developer and advocate for mental wellbeing, she focuses on building intuitive, student-friendly experiences that bring real support to the people who need it most.
